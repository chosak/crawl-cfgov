CFPB Financial Education Exchange (CFPB FinEx)

CFPB FinEx is a place where financial educators, practitioners, counselors, researchers, and others can share information and best practices, learn from one another, advance their work, and see what CFPB is doing to help consumers.

Explore our training webinars to help you improve your practice, as well as our tools and resources that you can share with clients.

New Resources for Older Americans

Thursday, May 20, 2021, 2-3 p.m. ET

Join us on Thursday, May 20, 2021, 2-3 p.m. ET for a free webinar. May is Older American’s month and June 15 is World Elder Abuse Awareness Day. Join us for a presentation spotlighting resources that can help older adults make sound financial decisions and avoid financial exploitation. You’ll hear from our Office for Older Americans on new and existing resources for older adults, their family caregivers, aging service providers, and financial practitioners. We will also discuss free resources that you can download or order in bulk to share with others in your community.

Need webinar log-in information? The information is sent in advance via e-mail. Sign up to receive e-mails from CFPB FinEx. Or, send a request to CFPB_FinEx@cfpb.gov.
