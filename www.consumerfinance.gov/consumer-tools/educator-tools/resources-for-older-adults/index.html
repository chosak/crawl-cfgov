Working with older adults

We’ve gathered resources to help you, whether you serve older adults or family members managing the finances of a loved one.

The CFPB’s Office for Older Americans is here to help older adults make financial decisions with confidence.

Learn more about our free educational resources.

Tools and resources to use with older adults

Tools for later-life financial security

Browse guides and information to help older adults make sound financial decisions.

  • Explore resources for older adults

Protecting against fraud

Protecting savings and assets becomes increasingly important as people age. We have resources to help safeguard finances and protect older adults from financial exploitation.

  • Learn how to protect against fraud

Managing someone else's money

Millions of people manage money or property for someone unable to pay bills or make financial decisions. To help financial caregivers, we’ve released plain language guides.

  • Browse guides for financial caregivers

Protect your loved ones during the pandemic

Older adults face unique financial challenges during the coronavirus pandemic. Learn how you can be aware of and respond to scams, manage someone's money while separated from them, and help loved ones make tough choices prior to and during retirement.

  • See resources for older adults and their families
