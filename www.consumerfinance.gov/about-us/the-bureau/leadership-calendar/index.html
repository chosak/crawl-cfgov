Leadership calendar

The Bureau is committed to letting the public know how it's working for consumers every day by, among other things, providing a view into the workday of our senior leadership.

The Bureau posts calendars online after reviewing them to determine whether any information contained therein must or should be withheld from public disclosure in accordance with the Freedom of Information Act. The Bureau updates the calendars retrospectively for the months each individual has occupied a leadership role. The Bureau has also retained the calendars of people who have previously led the day-to-day work of the Bureau.

Due to the time-intensive preparation process, each month’s calendar will appear on this page at least a few weeks after that month has concluded. Calendars are provided as PDF documents that are searchable within a PDF viewer.
